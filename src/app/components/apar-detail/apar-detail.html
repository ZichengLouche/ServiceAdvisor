Business Impact

Abstract
Db2 Eots Incorrectly aborted an in-commit UR After RRS was restarted and had done a resync with rrsaf.
Trigger
Db2 deferred End of Task processing may abort a DB2 UR that z/OS RRS was intending to commit.\nThis may occur if z/OS RRS is restarted and it has a UR that it intends to commit, but the Db2 application is cancelled or terminates before z/OS RRS is able to drive the Db2 RRSAF Commit Exit."
Local Fix
N/A
Users Affected
All Db2 11 & Db2 12 for z/os users of PITR(Point in time recovery) restart
Summary
During Db2 PITR restart, any inflight thread(Unit of Recovery) that requires to be backed out, may abend in DSNIARPL equal 5008. This abend should be avoided because we know this thread will be recovered later by RESTORE utility.
Conclusion
The logic in DSNIARPL is changed to avoid dsniarpl:5008 abend during PITR restart.
